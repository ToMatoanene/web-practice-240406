Introduction to Kinki Region

Kinki Region is one of the geographic areas in Japan. It is located in the center of the main island Honshu.

There are 7 prefectures in Kinki Region. Here is the list of them.

  • Osaka
  • Kyoto
  • Hyogo
  • Nara
  • Shiga
  • Wakayama
  • Mie*

*Notes; Mie is not included into Kinki Region in some election systems.
